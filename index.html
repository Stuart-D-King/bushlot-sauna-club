What sets us apart

Heritage. Craftsmanship. Mobility.

I

Heritage

Rooted in a Scandinavian sauna tradition passed down through generations, Bushlot saunas carry a story that mass-market brands cannot replicate. Every build begins with that inheritance.

II

Craftsmanship

Tongue-and-groove joinery, premium wood species, and careful attention to every detail — inside and out. You are acquiring a piece of functional art, not an appliance off a warehouse shelf.

III

Mobility

Built on a 5×10 utility trailer, your sauna goes where you go. Tow it to the lakeside, the campsite, or position it in the backyard. No permits, no fixed footprint, no limits.
