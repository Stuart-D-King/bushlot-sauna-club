The product

The Bushlot Sauna

A four-person mobile sauna built on a 5×10 trailer — handcrafted, all-inclusive, and ready to go wherever the season calls.

Overview

Built to be used, not admired from a distance.

Each Bushlot sauna is a handcrafted, four-person cabin built on a 5×10 utility trailer. It can be towed by a standard pickup truck or SUV, stored in a garage, and positioned at any location accessible by road — your backyard, a lakeside lot, a remote campsite.

The design is intentional and restrained: customers choose from a curated menu of options — stove type, interior wood species, exterior finish — rather than an infinite configurator. This keeps the product coherent, the brand honest, and every build a considered piece of craftsmanship rather than a commodity.

Make it yours

Your choices. Our craft.

Three meaningful decisions shape your sauna. Each option is curated — the palette is limited by design, so every build that leaves the workshop carries the same standard of quality.

01 — Stove

Heat Source

Choose a wood-burning or electric sauna stove from a curated selection at different performance and price tiers. Both options are fully integrated into the build — no aftermarket fit required.

02 — Interior Wood

Interior Wood

Select from premium pine, aromatic cedar, or thermally modified wood — each chosen for its thermal properties, natural aroma, and aesthetic quality. The interior is your sanctuary; the material matters.

03 — Exterior

Exterior Finish

A curated palette of exterior stain and paint colors keeps the aesthetic clean and coherent while giving you meaningful personalization. Your sauna, your mark on it.
